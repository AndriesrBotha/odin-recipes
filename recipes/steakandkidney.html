Steak and Kidney Pie

A SMALL AND YET FILLING DELICACY

Steak and kidney pie is a savoury pie that is filled principally with a mixture of diced beef, diced kidney, fried onion, and brown gravy. Steak and kidney pie is a representative dish of British cuisine

INGRIDIENTS

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Lamb kidney
  • Flour

Method

  1. Place steak and kidney in large pan with onions, water and crumbled stock cube
  2. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer covered 1½ hours
  3. Combine flour with extra water, pepper and soy sauce.
  4. Add to steak and stir until mixture re-boils and thickens
  5. If making a pie top cool the meat mixture and pour into an ovenproof dish
  6. Make small cuts in pastry edge creating a scalloped effect
  7. Make two slits in top of pastry to allow steam to escape.
  8. Bake in a hot oven (220°C) 10 minutes
  9. SERVE AND ENJOY
